## Create an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Babbily">
    Go to Babbily and choose **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a sign-up method">
    Continue with a supported social sign-in option or enter your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    If Babbily sends a confirmation code, enter the code from your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish account setup">
    Add your name, confirm the required account details, and complete the onboarding screens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start your first chat">
    After setup, open a new chat and send your first message.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Babbily may ask you to confirm that you meet the minimum age requirement before continuing.
</Note>

## Sign in

Use the same method you used to create your account. If you signed up with a social sign-in option, choose that option again. If you created a password for email sign-in, use your email address and password.

If Babbily says the account was not found, check that you entered the same email address you used during sign-up. If Babbily says the account already exists, switch to **Log In** instead of **Sign Up**.

## Reset your password

If you use email and password sign-in:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recovery flow">
    Choose **Forgot your password?** from the sign-in page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Use the email address on your Babbily account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Enter the reset code Babbily sends you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new password">
    Choose a new password and return to sign-in when Babbily confirms the update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you signed up with a social sign-in option and did not add a password, continue with that sign-in option or use the account settings flow when available.

## Accept a team invite

Team invitations are sent by email. Open the invitation link from the account you want to use with the team.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invite link">
    Use the latest invite email from your team owner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in or create an account">
    If prompted, sign in. New users may need to finish onboarding before accepting the invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the invitation">
    Review the team name, then choose **Accept invitation**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm access">
    After accepting, open Babbily and check **Settings > Team**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you use multiple email addresses, ask the team owner which email they invited before creating a new account.
</Tip>

## Manage account settings

Open **Settings > Account** to review account details, update available profile fields, request password help, or log out. For data you cannot remove yourself, account deletion requests, or access issues that remain after troubleshooting, contact support.
