Babbily plans control your monthly API usage budget, billing cycle, support level, and team features.

## Plan options

| Plan | Best for |
| --- | --- |
| Free | Trying Babbily and lightweight usage. |
| Personal | Individual work with a larger monthly budget. |
| Premium | Individual work with more usage and priority benefits. |
| Ultra | High-usage individual workflows, including more frequent media or research work. |
| Team | Shared workspace and billing for teams. |
| Teams Ultra | Higher team usage for larger or heavier workflows. |

## Open billing settings

Go to **Settings > Billing** to review your current plan, usage, renewal timing, and billing controls.

Depending on your account and plan, you may be able to:

- Upgrade.
- Downgrade.
- Cancel.
- Open the billing portal.
- Manage team subscription details.

## Team billing

Team plans use seat-based billing with a three-person minimum, including the owner. Accepted members receive team plan access and API usage budget. Pending invitations do not receive access or usage, but may affect the billed seat count until accepted, declined, or canceled.

Team owners manage the subscription. Team members can use their team entitlement but may not see owner-only billing controls.

Team plan cards may include shared workspace features, centralized billing and user management, team analytics or usage visibility, SSO integration, and priority support. Availability and setup can depend on the plan and account.

## Changing plans

Plan changes can affect:

- Available monthly usage.
- Renewal or reset timing.
- Team member usage.
- Access to plan-specific features.

Some changes apply immediately. Others may be scheduled for the next billing period.

<Tip>
  Check [API usage budget](/features/usage-budget) before heavy research or media generation so you know how much room remains in the current period.
</Tip>
