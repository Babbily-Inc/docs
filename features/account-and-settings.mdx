Use **Settings** to manage your Babbily account, workspace preferences, and connected features.

## Open settings

Select your account menu, then choose **Settings**.

Available tabs can depend on your account, plan, team role, and device.

## Settings tabs

| Tab | What you can manage |
| --- | --- |
| Profile | Name, nickname, location, and profile details. |
| Appearance | Display preferences. |
| Account | Account-related controls. |
| Privacy | Privacy-related preferences and account help. |
| Billing | Plan, usage, renewal, and billing controls. |
| Team | Team members, invitations, and team status. |
| Connectors | Connected apps and authorization status. |
| Skills | Verified skills and your markdown skills. |
| Memory | Memory Profile, saved context, citations, and memory controls. |

## When a setting is missing

A setting may be hidden when:

- You are signed out.
- Your plan does not include that feature.
- You are a team member and the control belongs to the team owner.
- The feature is unavailable on your current device or browser.

<Note>
  If you recently changed plans, joined a team, or connected an app, refresh settings after a moment.
</Note>
