News helps you browse current stories by category and open source-backed articles inside Babbily.

## Browse news

Open **News** from the sidebar. Babbily shows a feed based on your selected category and location settings.

Available categories include:

- Top.
- Tech & Science.
- Finance.
- Arts & Culture.
- Sports.
- Entertainment.

## Open an article

Select a story to open the full article view. Babbily can generate a readable article from the available headline, summary, and sources.

Articles can include:

- A generated article body.
- Source citations.
- An image when one is available.
- The story category.

## Location and personalization

Your location setting can help Babbily show more relevant news. You can review your location in **Settings > Profile**.

If you do not see local news, update your location and refresh News.

## Usage impact

Generating a full article uses AI work and can be subject to your API usage budget. News article generation can also have separate rate limits.

<Note>
  News browsing and generated articles are meant to help you understand current stories quickly. Always verify important decisions with the original sources.
</Note>
