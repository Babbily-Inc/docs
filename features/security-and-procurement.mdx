## Common procurement questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Babbily train models on my chats by default?">
    Babbily's privacy settings state that Babbily does not use your chats to train generative models by default unless you give explicit permission.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can we use SSO?">
    Team plan cards may list SSO integration. Availability and setup can depend on the plan and account. Contact support if SSO is a procurement requirement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can admins control connected apps?">
    Connector access depends on the account that authorizes the connected app and the permissions available in that app. Workspace admins should also manage app access from the connected app's own admin settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can users delete data?">
    Users can delete supported saved items, manage memory, disconnect apps where controls are available, and contact support for account-level deletion or data they cannot remove themselves.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What support information should we prepare?">
    Include the plan you are considering, expected number of users, billing owner email, SSO needs, privacy questions, support expectations, and required timeline.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Information to collect before contacting support

| Area | Helpful details |
| --- | --- |
| Organization | Company name, billing contact, expected user count, and rollout timeline. |
| Access | Whether you need SSO, team ownership, user management, or procurement approval. |
| Data | Approved use cases, restricted data types, connector expectations, and deletion requirements. |
| Billing | Preferred plan, team size, purchase timing, and invoice or subscription needs. |
| Support | Required support level, onboarding needs, and internal launch date. |

## Sensitive information boundaries

Do not include passwords, verification codes, API keys, private keys, payment card numbers, raw connected-app credentials, or confidential files in procurement emails or support tickets unless Babbily support specifically requests them through an approved support channel.

<Tip>
  If your organization requires specific legal documents, security questionnaires, or vendor review forms, contact support with the request and the review deadline.
</Tip>
