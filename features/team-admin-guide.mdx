Use this guide if you own or help manage a Babbily team workspace.

<Note>
  Team controls can vary by plan, role, billing state, and account state. Owner-only controls may be hidden for team members.
</Note>

## Team roles

| Role | What they can usually do |
| --- | --- |
| Team owner | Manage the workspace, invitations, member removal, plan changes, billing, and team usage. |
| Team member | Use the team plan, view team information, and work in Babbily with their own account. |

Team members may see plan and usage information, but subscription changes and owner-level billing actions belong to the team owner.

## Create a team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a team plan">
    Choose **Team** or **Teams Ultra** from the plan flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the workspace">
    Add the team or company name customers should recognize in settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add teammates">
    Invite enough people to meet the setup requirement. Team setup requires at least three people total, including the owner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete subscription setup">
    Finish the billing step so the workspace can become active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invite members

Owners can invite one or more teammates by email from **Settings > Team**.

After an invitation is sent:

- Pending invitations can be reviewed from team settings.
- Invitees join after accepting the invitation.
- Accepted members receive access to the team plan and team API usage budget.
- Pending invitations do not receive team access or usage until accepted.

## Manage members and invitations

Use **Settings > Team** to review accepted members and pending invitations. Depending on your role and the invitation state, you may be able to resend, cancel, or remove access.

<Warning>
  Removing a member stops that person from receiving team plan access. It does not delete content inside the member's separate account or connected apps.
</Warning>

## Billing and plan changes

Team owners manage billing from **Settings > Billing**. Team members should ask the owner to make plan changes, update billing, or troubleshoot missing subscription controls.

If a recent billing change is not visible yet, refresh settings and check again after a short wait.

## Team API usage budget

Team plans include API usage budget for accepted users. Team members follow the team plan's reset timing.

If a team workspace runs out of included usage:

- Team members should contact the team owner.
- Team owners can review usage from billing and usage surfaces.
- Team owners can upgrade when the plan supports it.

## Troubleshooting team controls

If a team control is missing:

- Confirm whether you are the team owner.
- Confirm the invitation was accepted.
- Refresh **Settings > Team** and **Settings > Billing**.
- Ask the team owner to manage billing or subscription changes.
- Contact support if the expected control still does not appear.
