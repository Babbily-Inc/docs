Use this FAQ when you are changing plans, reviewing usage, or helping a team understand billing visibility.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I manage billing?">
    Open **Settings > Billing**. Depending on your account, role, and plan, you may see your current plan, usage, renewal timing, upgrade or downgrade actions, cancel controls, or a billing portal link.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What changes when I upgrade?">
    Upgrading can increase your monthly API usage budget and unlock plan-specific features. Some changes may apply quickly, while others can take a short time to appear across usage and billing surfaces.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What changes when I downgrade or cancel?">
    Downgrades or cancellations can reduce future usage, change renewal timing, or remove plan-specific features. Downgrades are typically scheduled for the end of the current billing period, so you keep your current plan until then and are not charged at the time you schedule the change.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will Babbily confirm before charging me?">
    Yes. Selecting an upgrade, downgrade, or cancel-downgrade action on the pricing page opens a confirmation dialog that explains what happens next, including whether you are charged now or at the next renewal. No billing change is made until you confirm. Checkout and the billing portal show their own confirmation screens.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel a scheduled downgrade?">
    Yes. If you have a downgrade scheduled for the end of the billing period, the matching plan card shows a **Cancel Downgrade** action. Selecting it opens a confirmation dialog and, once confirmed, keeps you on your current plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When does usage reset?">
    Individual plans reset on the usage period shown in your account. Paid plans usually align with the billing period. Team members follow the timing shown for the team plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does unused API usage budget roll over?">
    No. Unused API usage budget does not roll over to the next period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does team billing work?">
    Team plans use seat-based billing with a three-person minimum, including the owner. Accepted members receive team plan access and API usage budget. Pending invitations do not receive access or usage, but may affect the billed seat count until accepted, declined, or canceled.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why can I not see billing controls?">
    Billing controls can depend on your role. Team members may have limited billing visibility and should ask the team owner to manage plan or subscription changes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why did usage or billing not update immediately?">
    Usage and billing cards can take a short time to refresh after model work, media generation, team membership changes, or plan updates. Refresh settings and check again after a short wait.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What team features may be listed on plan cards?">
    Team plan cards may list shared workspace features, centralized billing and user management, team analytics or usage visibility, SSO integration, and priority support. Availability and setup can depend on your plan and account, so contact support if you need help confirming a specific requirement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What should I send support for a billing issue?">
    Include your account email, plan, whether you are a team owner or member, what you expected, what happened instead, and the approximate date, time, and timezone. Do not send full payment card numbers or sensitive credentials.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
